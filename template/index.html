JOB FAIR '24

CANDIDATE REGISTRATION
EMPLOYER REGISTRATION

Pinnacle'24 will accure the employers, experienced professionals and entry level talented individuals under one roof. This Job fair will also compile organizations from various sectors including all kinds of private organizations, highly competitive and professional job seekers thereby serving as the platform for an open and wide-ranging employment opportunities.

Employment is one of the basic needs in order to survive in the world. Employers and job seekers will be available in one place and the job seekers can meet the employers and get the chance to get their dream job. The main objective of Pinnacle 2024 is to provide employment to the unemployed youths of Kerala. Through this job fest, the job seekers can meet the employers and be able to grab the job of their choice.

ENTREPRENEURS Help Desk

Mizone is providing a free help desk service for all the entrepeneurs including startups, medium, small and micro enterprises (MSMEs).The services include clearing doubts, offering advisory services on different areas of the business such as organization registration, statutory taxes, licenses, approvals from Government/Local Self Government Institutions, various loans/schemes and financial/marketing/operational/ taxation, introducing latest technology etc. A panel of experts in various sectors will address the queries from the entrepreneurs.
